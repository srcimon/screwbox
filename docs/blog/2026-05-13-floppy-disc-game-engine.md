Hi there,

starting from 3.29.0 ScrewBox will no longer require any transitive dependency.
Previous versions of ScrewBox relied on Jackson to deal with Tiles Map editor maps and tilesets.
This reduces the total amount of storage for the engine from 2.1 to 1.5mb.
ScrewBox is now officially just one floppy disk away from fitting into your retro build!
